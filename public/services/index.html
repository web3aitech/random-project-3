Services

Beyond production

Engineering services for GRP pipeline projects

In addition to the production and supply of GRP pipes and fittings, Dubai Pipes Factory services extend to:

Pipe design verification

Using AWWA M45 mainly for underground pipe installation, and Caesar II for above-ground installation.

Pre-recommendation

Recommendations on the type of piping material and properties best suited to your application.

Site installation

For coupling joint or lamination joint, delivered by experienced site crews.

Inspection & supervision

Site inspection and supervision of installation to ensure correct jointing and alignment.

Deflection verification

Verification of pipe deflection after installation to confirm the pipeline meets specification.

Specifying a GRP pipeline?

Get a quote or talk to our engineering team about diameters, pressure ratings and installation method.

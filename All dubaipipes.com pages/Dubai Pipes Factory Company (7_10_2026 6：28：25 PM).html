Welcome to Dubai Pipes Factory Co.

Dubai Pipes plant is for the production and supply of Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP ) Pipes, Joints, Fittings, Manhole Liners, Cover Slabs, and Tanks. GRP pipes are produced either by the either the Continuous winding process, as per the Know How and Technology plus machines supplied by Flowtite Technology A S; or by the Dual Helical winding process.

Dubai Pipes Factory Co. produces pipes with a diameter range up to 2400 mm, and pressure rating up to 32 bar. GRP pipe standard Specific Tangential Initial Stiffness includes: 2500, 5000 and 10000 N/m2. The manufactured standard pipe length is up to 12 m.

Our GRP pipes are in accordance with international standards like AWWA, BS EN and ASTM.

CERTIFICATIONS

DPFC is quality committed and ISO 9001:2008 accredited. A documented Quality Management System Manual confirms compliance with ISO standard. The manual is available upon request.

Conformity with international standard had been established by independent party –BSI- and satisfactory assessment of the manufacturing control process had taken place to establish that such conformity is consistently achieved. Accordingly the below Kitemark are obtained:

  • ASTM D 3262 Kitemark License: KM 505457
  • ASTM D 3517 Kitemark License: KM 505456
  • ASTM D 3754 Kitemark License: KM 505455
  • BS 5480 Kitemark License: KM 505454
  • BS EN 1796 Kitemark License: KM 549259
  • BS EN 14364 Kitemark License: KM 505454

DPFC products are approved by Water Regulations Advisory Scheme.

DPFC is committed to the Environmental Management system framework and requirements of the ISO
14001:2004 Standard.

APPROVALS

Products produced by Dubai Pipes factory are approved and in use in many of the middle east countries, GCC countries and specifically in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and the other Northern Emirates of the United Arabs emirates. Approvals had been received from most of the local and international contractors, project consultants and governmental authorities. A huge amount of pipes produced by Dubai Pipes are currently is use for mainly the sewerage, irrigation as well as storm water networks.
